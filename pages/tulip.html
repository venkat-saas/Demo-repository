History of the Tulip

Tulips were first cultivated in the Ottoman Empire (Turkey) in the 16th century. Later, they became extremely popular in the Netherlands, leading to the famous "Tulip Mania" in the 17th century.

Today, tulips are grown worldwide and symbolize love, beauty, and new beginnings. With their wide variety of colors, they are among the most admired flowers in the world.
