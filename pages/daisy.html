History of Daisy

Daisies are one of the most beloved flowers in the world, symbolizing innocence, purity, and new beginnings. They were cherished in Norse mythology as sacred to Freya, the goddess of love and fertility.

With their bright white petals and sunny yellow centers, daisies bring cheerfulness and positivity wherever they bloom.
