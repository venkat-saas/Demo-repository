The History of Rose

Roses are among the oldest cultivated flowers in human history. Archaeological evidence shows that roses have existed for over *35 million years*. They were first grown in gardens nearly *5,000 years ago* in China.

In Ancient Rome, roses were used in celebrations and also as a symbol of luxury. Today, the rose is called the **“Queen of Flowers”**, symbolizing love, passion, and beauty worldwide. Different colors carry different meanings:

  • ❤️ Red Rose – Love & Romance
  • 🤍 White Rose – Purity & Innocence
  • 💛 Yellow Rose – Friendship
  • 💗 Pink Rose – Gratitude & Admiration
