History of the Sunflower

Sunflowers originated in North America more than 4,500 years ago. They were cultivated by Native Americans for food, oil, and decoration.

Known for always turning towards the sun, the sunflower symbolizes happiness, strength, and loyalty. Today, it is one of the most popular and loved flowers worldwide.
