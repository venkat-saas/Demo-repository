History of the Lily

Lilies are among the most ancient and symbolic flowers in the world. They have been cultivated for more than 3,000 years and were highly valued in ancient civilizations like Egypt and Greece.

In Greek mythology, lilies were associated with the goddess Hera, symbolizing motherhood and rebirth. Even today, lilies are seen as a flower of purity, devotion, and renewal.
