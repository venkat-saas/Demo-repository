🌺 About Our Flower Nursery 🌺

🌼 Who We Are 🌼

Welcome to the Flower Nursery — a special place where beauty meets knowledge. Our mission is to bring you closer to the magical world of flowers. From Roses to Orchids, every flower tells a story of love, history, and culture.

🌱 Our Mission 🌱

We aim to spread awareness about the importance of flowers in our lives. Whether it's a symbol of celebration, healing, or nature’s art — flowers make our world colorful and peaceful. 🌍✨

📖 Our Story 📖

This Flower Garden started as a small garden project in 2020. Today, it has grown into a complete online guide, where you can explore different flowers, learn their history, and even plan a visit to our Nursery. 🌸
