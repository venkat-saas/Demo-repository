History of Orchid

Orchids are among the oldest and largest families of flowering plants, with over 25,000 species worldwide. They symbolize love, strength, and luxury. Orchids have been cherished in China, Greece, and Aztec cultures for centuries.

With their delicate yet vibrant look, orchids are prized for elegance and are often gifted during special occasions.
